About Independence Day

Independence Day in India marks the end of British colonial rule and the birth of a free and sovereign nation. On August 15, 1947, India gained independence from British rule, which had lasted for almost 200 years. This day is celebrated annually as a national holiday and holds immense historical and cultural significance for Indians.Independence Day is not just a historical event but a celebration of the values and aspirations that continue to shape India's future. It is a reminder of the nation's journey from colonial subjugation to self-determination and its ongoing quest for progress and unity.
